About our bakery

Our bakery caters for all those who enjoy all the famous Austrian bakery delacacies including "Sachertorte" a delightful chocolate cake with apricot jam and fine chocolate icing created by the then 16 year old Franz Sacher (1816-1907). Our selection also naturally includes the Austrian Strudel cake made from apples, the wonderful cinnamon spice, raisins wrapped in a delicious delicate pastry.
